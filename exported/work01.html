These are the original design ideas for a coffee shop called The Darkest Roast. To the best of my knowledge this is not a real coffee shop, and any similarities are purely coincidental.

After implementing these designs into a responsive page I found that the changes from mobile to desktop were too jarring, and the final desktop design was too cluttered. I also felt the mobile design was too plain, so I incorporated a few of the desktop ideas into it. After simplifying the desktop design to be more like the mobile I found the flow from one screen size to the other was much better.
